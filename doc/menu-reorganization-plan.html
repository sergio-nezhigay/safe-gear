Summary of Changes

What Moved

  • Safety Signs ‚Üí Moved from top level into Fire Safety category under new "Signs & Marking" subcategory
  • Security Mirrors ‚Üí Changed from top-level category to subcategory under new "Surveillance & Security" parent
  • Product Security ‚Üí Changed from top-level category to subcategory under new "Surveillance & Security" parent

New Groupings Created

  • Surveillance & Security (parent category) - Logically combines Security Mirrors + Product Security into one security-focused section
  • Institutional Equipment (parent category) - Groups school-related bell systems and clocks together
  • Signs & Marking (subcategory) - Groups fire signs and safety signs under Fire Safety where they belong contextually

Key Benefits

  • Simplified Navigation: Top-level categories reduced from 6 to 5, making the menu less cluttered
  • Logical Grouping: Related products now grouped by function and use case (e.g., all surveillance products together)
  • Improved Discoverability: Customers looking for security products will find both mirrors and alarms in one place
  • Better Scalability: New structure makes it easier to add products in the future without creating more top-level clutter
  • Professional Organization: Category names clearly communicate the purpose and contents
