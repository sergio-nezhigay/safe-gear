Product Add-ons Quick Guide

5-Minute Setup Guide

SafeGear E-commerce Platform

What it does: Lets customers add accessories, services, and warranties to their purchase.

Two types: Quantity (for physical items) and Yes/No (for services).

üéØ Two Metafields, That's It

1. addon_products (Main Product)

Where: Main product metafields
Type: List of products
What: Which products show as add-ons

2. addon_type (Each Add-on Product)

Where: Each add-on product metafields
Values: "quantity" = Shows +/- buttons
"yes_no" = Shows checkbox only
Default: "quantity"

‚ö° Quick Setup

For Accessories (Shelves, Locks, etc.)

  1. Open add-on product ‚Üí Metafields ‚Üí Set addon_type = "quantity"
  2. Open main product ‚Üí Metafields ‚Üí Add to addon_products list
  3. Save. Done.

Result: Checkbox + quantity controls (- 1 +)

For Services (Warranty, Installation, etc.)

  1. Open add-on product ‚Üí Metafields ‚Üí Set addon_type = "yes_no"
  2. Open main product ‚Üí Metafields ‚Üí Add to addon_products list
  3. Save. Done.

Result: Checkbox only

üîß Common Issues

Problem Fix
Nothing shows up Check addon_products on main product has items
No +/- buttons Set addon_type = "quantity" on add-on product
Can't change quantity Change addon_type from "yes_no" to "quantity"
Too many add-ons Theme Customizer ‚Üí Limit to 3-6 products

‚úÖ Best Practices

Keep it simple:
  • 3-6 add-ons max per product
  • Order by importance (first = shows first)
  • Use clear product names
  • Mix quantity and yes/no types
  • Test before launching
